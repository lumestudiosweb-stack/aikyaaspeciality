About Us

Specialist Care, Close to Home

Aikyaa Speciality Clinic is a focused two-speciality practice in Bendoorwell, Mangaluru, bringing together an experienced Surgical Oncologist and a Psychiatrist under one roof. Our goal is to make high-quality specialist care accessible, affordable, and genuinely patient-centred.

Whether you are seeking expert guidance on a Cancer diagnosis or navigating Mental Health challenges, our doctors take the time to listen, evaluate thoroughly, and craft a Treatment Plan tailored specifically to you.

We are conveniently located near Bendoorwell Dominos Pizza, with easy access by public transport and ample parking for those coming by private vehicle.

Our Specialities

Focused expertise in Surgical Oncology and Psychiatry — two fields where the right specialist makes all the difference.

🎗️

Surgical Oncology

Expert Surgical management of Cancers with a focus on precision, organ preservation, and optimal Oncological outcomes guided by Dr. Karthik K S.

🦷

Oral Cancer

Diagnosis, Staging, and Surgical Treatment of Oral Cavity Cancers — including Resection and Reconstructive Procedures for best functional outcomes.

🌸

Breast Surgery & Lumpectomy

Breast-conserving Surgeries and Lumpectomy Procedures for early-stage Breast Cancer, tailored to each patient's clinical and personal needs.

🔬

Gynaecological Oncology

Surgical Treatment of Ovarian Cancer and other Gynaecological Malignancies, performed with meticulous care and evidence-based technique.

🧠

Psychiatry & Mental Health

Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluation and Treatment for adults and adolescents by Dr. Krithishree S S., MD — with empathy at the centre of every Consultation.

💬

Anxiety & Depression

Evidence-based management of Anxiety Disorders, Depression, and Mood Conditions through a combination of Medication and structured Psychotherapy.

🌙

Stress & Sleep Disorders

Assessment and Treatment of Chronic Stress, Burnout, Insomnia, and Sleep-related Psychiatric Conditions affecting daily quality of life.

🤝

De-addiction & Rehabilitation

Supportive Psychiatric Care for Substance Use Disorders, with a focus on Recovery, Relapse Prevention, and long-term Mental Wellness.
